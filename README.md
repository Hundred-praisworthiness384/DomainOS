DomainOS is a local-first app built to help you manage your business knowledge. It works on your Windows PC without sending data to the cloud. Use it to search your documents, run workflows, and integrate Gmail and Google Tasks. It supports several AI models like Claude, GPT, and Ollama.

---

## 📥 How to Download and Install DomainOS on Windows

1. Click the large green **Download DomainOS** button at the top. It will take you to the GitHub page where you get the app.

2. The GitHub page shows the latest version in the "Releases" section. Look for the latest Windows installer file, ending in `.exe`. It might be named like `DomainOS-Setup.exe`.

3. Click the `.exe` file name to download it to your computer.

4. Once it finishes, open the file by double-clicking it in your Downloads folder.

5. The setup wizard will open. Follow the prompts by clicking **Next**.

6. Choose the folder where you want DomainOS installed or use the default.

7. Click **Install** to begin.

8. When the installer finishes, you can start DomainOS from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.

## 🚀 Getting Started with DomainOS

After opening DomainOS for the first time:

1. **Set up your workspace.** You will be asked to link your business domains or knowledge files to the app. You can add folders from your computer or connect your Gmail and Google Tasks accounts.

2. **Pick your AI model.** DomainOS supports Claude, GPT, and Ollama. You can switch between them to find what works best for you.

3. **Try a search.** Enter keywords or questions to search your knowledge base using semantic search technology.

4. **Create missions.** Organize tasks and workflows with approval steps to keep your team aligned.

5. **Explore integrations.** Link your Google services for easy task and email management.

## 🔧 Features You Will Use

- **Local data storage:** All your information stays on your machine unless you choose to share it.  
- **Semantic search:** Find relevant information across all your documents, not just by keywords.  
- **Multi-AI support:** Use different AI providers to get varied results without leaving the app.  
- **Mission workflows:** Plan and track tasks with built-in approval gates to manage business processes.  
- **Google services integration:** Connect Gmail and Google Tasks to combine emails and to-dos in one place.  
- **Private and secure:** No cloud storage unless you add it, so your data remains yours.

## ⚙️ How to Use DomainOS Safely

- Keep your Windows updated to get security patches.  
- Run the installer from the official GitHub link only.  
- Back up your important documents before adding them to DomainOS.  
- Use strong passwords for your Gmail and Google accounts when linking.   
- Review permissions the app requests during setup.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Tips

- If the installer does not start, check that your PC meets system requirements.  
- Disable antivirus temporarily if it blocks the setup but enable it after.  
- If the app won’t open, restart your computer and try again.  
- For issues with AI models not responding, check your internet connection.  
- If Gmail or Google Tasks integration fails, confirm your login details and permissions.
